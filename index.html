About

HF Medical Associates

HF Medical Associates was founded in 1995 as a family practice medical service. We have grown and expand our service, providing a full range of support services for patients of different demographics.

HF Medical Associates focus in the care as many of the providers in South Florida but with the time and attention you deserve. Here Our main specialty is to serve you in a humanized way, with agility, efficiency and in an accessible way.

The service is provided in individual offices, with all the privacy, respect and attention that the patient deserves.
